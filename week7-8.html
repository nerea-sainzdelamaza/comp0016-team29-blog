Week 7-8

  • 5 Jul 2022

During this period, we took some time to revisit the requirements of the Microsoft Store and realized that some adjustments needed to be made to our codebase. Specifically, we needed to move the executable files of both MotionInput and MFC to the root folder of our submission to ensure that the applications were properly installed and accessible to users. We worked diligently to implement these changes and ensure that our application met all of the necessary requirements for submission to the Microsoft Store.

At the same time, we continued to work on finalising our MFC templates, which would be used by the other 17 teams during term 2. We took great care to ensure that the templates were comprehensive, easy to use, and compatible with the various MotionInput products. Through this effort, we aim to provide the teams with a valuable resource that will facilitate their development process and help them overcome any potential challenges they might encounter.
