Week 11-12

  • 5 Jul 2022

During the first two weeks of term 2, our team started contacting each of our assigned MotionInput teams to inform them that we are available to help with any MFC, MotionInput, or compilation bugs they may encounter. We wanted to make sure that all teams knew that they could rely on us for support and assistance. Additionally, we introduced the MFC templates that we had created to the MotionInput teams. We started by working with Team 25 and Team 26, who were the first teams to require this support. We helped them navigate the codebase and understand how to use the templates effectively.

During this time, we also prepared and delivered our elevator pitch on January 20th. We had the opportunity to showcase our progress to the clients and explain our products so far. Our goal was to convey the value of our work and demonstrate how our efforts would contribute to the success of the overall project. Through effective communication and collaboration, we were able to ensure that all teams were well-informed and supported throughout the development process.
